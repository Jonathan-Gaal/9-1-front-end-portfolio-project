Enter the House of Wisdom!

Welcome to Jonathan Gaal's House of Wisdom, where the past meets the present. My favorite way to do things and live tends towards the proverbial "old-fashioned-way." But the modern age lays claim upon us all. The above sentiment is the inspiration for this project. The content of the pages splayed across the browser are rooted in the past. And yet the lines of this "digital poetry" of sorts, is written in HTML, CSS, and Javascript -- truly the modern language of the web.
